# Chapter 35: Bibliography

## Core Idea
Key references cited throughout the GROMACS manual, organized by topic. Cite the appropriate paper(s) when publishing results obtained with GROMACS.

## Primary GROMACS References
- **GROMACS 4.5/5**: Abraham et al., SoftwareX, 1-2, 19-25 (2015) — main GROMACS citation
- **GROMACS GPU acceleration**: Kutzner et al., J. Comput. Chem., 36, 1990-2008 (2015)
- **GROMACS 2020+**: Páll et al., J. Chem. Phys., 153, 134110 (2020)

## Algorithm References
- **PME**: Essmann et al., J. Chem. Phys., 103, 8577 (1995)
- **LINCS**: Hess et al., J. Comput. Chem., 18, 1463 (1997)
- **SETTLE**: Miyamoto & Kollman, J. Comput. Chem., 13, 952 (1992)
- **V-rescale**: Bussi et al., J. Chem. Phys., 126, 014101 (2007)
- **Parrinello-Rahman**: Parrinello & Rahman, J. Appl. Phys., 52, 7182 (1981)
- **Nose-Hoover**: Hoover, Phys. Rev. A, 31, 1695 (1985)
- **BAR**: Bennett, J. Comput. Phys., 22, 245 (1976)
- **MBAR**: Shirts & Chodera, J. Chem. Phys., 129, 124105 (2008)
- **AWH**: Lindahl et al., J. Chem. Phys., 159, 234110 (2023)

## Force Field References
- **AMBER ff99SB-ILDN**: Lindorff-Larsen et al., Proteins, 78, 1950 (2010)
- **CHARMM36**: Huang & MacKerell, J. Comput. Chem., 34, 2135 (2013)
- **GROMOS 54A7**: Schmid et al., Eur. Biophys. J., 40, 843 (2011)
- **OPLS-AA**: Jorgensen et al., JACS, 118, 11225 (1996)

## Key Takeaways
1. Cite Abraham et al. (2015) as the primary GROMACS reference
2. Cite Kutzner et al. (2015) for GPU-accelerated simulations
3. Cite the relevant method paper for specific algorithms used
4. Check the manual for the most up-to-date citation recommendations
